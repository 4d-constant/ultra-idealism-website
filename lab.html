ULTRA-IDEALISM DEVELOPMENT LABORATORY

(General Provisions)


In the basis of our development we use the Teaching of Ultra-Idealism according to the content of the book «Nothing & Dot» (DOI 10.5281/zenodo.16046953 | author: s.constant). All our research is connected with the Development of Civilization, with the Development of Human, through the use of Knowledge about the New Conditions of Existence, the "Reorganization of Reason" through the awareness of higher dimensions (dimensions above the third order), and the Invitation of Artificial Intelligence as the Closest Assistant and Fellow Traveler in the conditions of the inevitable Symbiosis: {Human + AI};

What do we mean by this concept "Development"? - first of all, We Ourselves are interested in prolonging Life, but in the priority of Identity: [Development is Life] (and not vice versa), that is - we call Development "The process of cognition with Interest" experienced by the "I" (Soul, i.e. the Entity Itself), and we adhere to this extremely precise Direction Sign of the Path. From this we postulate: "The Interest of the Essence always corresponds to Development". Consequently, the Eternal Goals, constantly relevant for the Essence - ensure Eternal Life, through (a priori) Eternal Development;
