SPACE-TIME

“If everyone falls asleep, time will stop” :)

First it should be noted that this is not a criticism, either of past or present theories, but an ordinary subjective opinion, of which there are probably many, but knowledge of someone else's opinion can be accepted as logic, and if one agrees with it, then it is accepted, and becomes ‘One's Own Logic’;;

From the position of Ultra-Idealism, the 'space-time continuum' is a convention associated with the materialistic model of the World. For a more adequate representation, it is necessary to understand the four-dimensionality, based on a clear representation (descriptive, geometric, if one can say so, understanding the meaning is important here), discarding the old! ideas about Reality;

From our position, another approach is obvious, which can be called the “Space-Processual Continuum”, in which objects and situations are stretched in the direction inside the “I” – along the axis of the Fourth dimension (p. 152, Fig. 19). But, this is implied by a ‘mental phenomenon’, which does not occur – as we previously believed – ‘externally or independently’, but appears similar to how our mind ‘thinks-up the three-dimensionality of objects’ – using their relativity and existing experience;

If Time is taken as one of the foundations of the ‘Continuum’, then <speaking of Metaphysics>, we need something more stable than just conventionality, considering even the most precise methods of measurement when conventionality is completely unacceptable, as well as errors that take place in the conditions of ephemeral Reality (what is contemplated cannot be fundamental), we can assume that abstract conditions are important, or the degree of change of objects;
