HOPE ("WAITING FOR A MIRACLE")


There are no accidents – turn off the gaming processes in real life, although such a belief is fashionable: “Life is a Game”, do not joke, if you did not intentionally intend to ‘walk on the edge of a knife’ - do not hope for a random ‘Success as a Gift’ - remember the difference between the concepts: “to believe” and “to know”. The world will provide everything you need, and believe – it will be even better than what you dreamed of;

"A man has an excess of what he does not need: a farmer has much apples, a rich man – lot of money, a beggar has a mountain of junk and thousands of cockroaches"

A strong desire will strengthen the opposition of the "Entity", with the intention of forcing development - Ah! You want it so much? It is too valuable for You – So, then You will have to achieve it :) And, notice after this, how you achieve such trifles, empty trinkets that have no value;

Therefore, do not wish too much, the "Entity" will never forget to Give what will belong to you. And the more often and more you yourself intend to ‘Give’, with a feeling of sincere care for strangers, and namely ‘Give to Society’ - especially valuable is that which will affect the development of many people, without the slightest hope for 'Gifts of Destiny', in response to your actions - the more will be repaid (that is, will return from everyone whom it touched, but to your address). Do not hope, do not wait, for a random gift and, do not wish for anything, also do not endow with value, except for the life process - knowledge and exp. (except for Development - it is valuable);

If you received a Gift, do not try to guess the Reason, just Accept it with Gratitude (“This is from God to you”);
