IDENTITY OF PERSONALITY


The identity of the “past I” to the “current I” lies in the impossibility to setting of another “I”, because the wave containing the experience of a specific being contains the “Essence of Self-identification”, and “Self-identification itself" is also an experience, that is, a fact of acquired knowledge, confirmed with each relation and experience;

Каждый раз при обращении внимания к Себе через многие сторонние отношения (пространственные и свойственные) этот опыт укрепляется, становится еще более стабильным, интегрируясь в события и взаимоотношения с объектами. И в отношениях с другими людьми – Самоидентификация осуществляется посредством взаимной относительности;

Each time when paying attention to the Self through many external relations (spatial and inherent), this experience is strengthened, becomes more stable, integrating into events and relations with objects. And in relations with other people - Self-identification is carried out through mutual relativity. Paying attention to the Essence of this Act of identification, which occurs fleetingly (motorically) and unnoticeably, one can understand that the “Real aspect of the Entity” – reading the wave of any being, will recognize “Itself as Itself”, based on the content of the wave. The “Essence” itself will not change from this. In case of obvious madness, another (living) entity deliberately penetrates the wave, capturing another's body, how does this happen? - we don't know yet.

The individual "I" contained in one specific wave is absolutely unique and static (wave), and no matter which of the "Real aspects of the Entity" reads this wave - the specific individuality of the "I" will not suffer, even if after each state of sleep "Real aspects" turn out to be different. We found out that the "Global aspect of the Entity" provides "His Own I" as an infinite number of "Real aspects of the I", this can be either a "Single Distributed I", or the "Great Single I" branches into an infinite number of precisely separate "I" - this will not change either the Meaning, or the Functionality, or especially the "Self-identification";

We have discussed this problem "Is it my own 'I'?", the answer is unambiguous: the "I" of every living being, as a Personality is Absolutely Unique, Eternal and Indestructible. The "Real Aspect of the Entity" ensures its (of "Concrete 'I' ") activity in the conditions of reality, this is a reliable way to store the experience and the Essence of Self-identification in a static wave (Remember: 'Everyone is offered Peace and Eternity');

It is impossible to establish the detailed structure of the Entity (God the Father, for example), just as, when researching in real conditions, we cannot determine whether the electrons in atoms are different – by comparing physical mass and tension, since this is a manifestation of the wave function – the Only One in the Whole World <… material electrons do not exist, even in an ephemeral form?>. If particles are realized, then – the Uniqueness of each of them is embedded in the Unified Function of the Electron, with the expectation that someone will still find a way to compare them physically, both in the only World of the researcher himself, and in different ones, if this is a group of people, and then, in this case, the Condition of Uniqueness is realized;

"The Essence of Self-Identification" is precisely That which one wants to preserve for the continuation of life, since this content is the only link of correspondence between a specific Personality and Its experience. The loss of the "Essence of Self-Identification" (recognition of Oneself), or another method of correct Identification (with loss of one's name, which is possible), is equivalent to the End of Life, or Death;

“Death of a Being” is a statement of the fact of the loss of the Prospect of Development;

We suggest not to define “Life” as “Development”, but it should be known that “Development” – we call “Life”, is a ‘Fact of Current Existence’ which is predetermined by Development, and if the Basis is absent, then the Consequences also disappear;
