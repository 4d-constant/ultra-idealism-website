LAZINESS


Laziness - is a social and psychological pathology that demonstrates the absence of a rational worldview (complete apathy, no goal, no development plan). This is a very dangerous disease that can destroy, literally ‘crush a person’;

Laziness must be fought at the level of the State Healthcare System, because it entails Economic Damage of cumulative action. Each person is capable of immediately, independently getting rid of this ailment, although, most likely, he will not take this decisive step, and will remain lying in a semi-dark room on a dusty sofa - risking everything, both health and life;

The Entity has many tools for various purposes, and Laziness is one of the toughest: Don't you want to develop? Don't you have any Plans? - then lie down, grow fat, the disease will find you, through your dangerous thoughts, i.e. the threat will not leave you alone, and this is a completely standard pattern of thoughts that sooner or later begin to haunt the lazy person, and he will no longer be able to think about development;

If you detect Laziness, urgently look for Interest, otherwise you will have to part with many values, first of all, this is appearance, immunity will weaken, the psyche will be disturbed, and therefore soon, you will draw the rest yourself;

Any sincere interest eliminates Laziness. It will disappear as if it never existed, strength will be found immediately, energy for get knowledge and actions will appear (-You will come to life). If Interest absorbs you, then goals will soon be formed, maybe you'll able to see the Global Purpose, then will be a meaning to live long, or Eternally (depend on the reason);

Workaholics – blame themselves for relaxing in vain. I dare to reassure them – a short sleep (rest) after active actions is very necessary, as it will be compensated by high productivity, but perhaps this is not the most important thing, what is important is a ‘reboot’ after sleep, which will entail a change in reality in connection with a personal contribution to the development of the “Entity”. Based on the changes in the new reality, it is probably possible to learn to qualify previous actions, from the position of the “Entity Development System”, as a result – we will be able to better navigate when choosing “goals and paths” (including the Global Purpose);
