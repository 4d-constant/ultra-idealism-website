ABOUT WORLDVIEW


The very First Question. It is so tricky, it activates the Idea of reporting to Society, in Itself, hidden, secretly, it is necessary to force It to be taken into account. Meanwhile, this is, without exaggeration, the Most Important Question – worrying 'almost'* every person in the World known to us – on Earth (and not only), and this Question is asked exactly like this:

Do you believe in the existence of God?

There is one necessary side of this question, which can greatly influence the degree of subjective importance and excitement, awe, contained in it. That side, again subjectively, endows God with some qualities. In the imagination of each He is different, we ourselves endow Him with far-fetched properties, about which we have a subjective idea.

Without going deep into psyche, for an adequate judgment, and for a positive decision in the question of "Faith in the Existence of God", in addition to such an egoistic question - "Do I want God to Exist at all?", we need to understand whether He is endowed with that Omnipresence?, that is, simultaneous access to thoughts and feelings, not to mention the actions, opinions and judgments towards other people.

Does He know about My own attitude towards Myself?

Can anything be hidden from God? – something I have never told anyone. Does He see me now? Does He know my thoughts? Does He feel what I feel right now?

There is only one answer to all these questions: "Yes, He is" (He Can), and we are talking specifically about the "Real aspect of the Global 'I'" - since we are not able to adequately reason about "God the Father" (here we call Him the "Global aspect of the Entity") - He is active, but abstractly, in the multifacetedness of an infinite degree, providing* His Own "I", to every alive being in the World, in the infinite Universe. Only the most general understanding is available to us, but in the conditions of Reality we are limited in ability ;

*The providing, by the “Global aspect of the Entity”, of Its Own “I” to every being in the Universe, does not happen by Its desire, that is, intentionally – this is Its feature that arose at the moment of the Firstoccurrence, this is the continuity in the fundamental relativity [ “Entity” | “Consciousness” ] – His Nature;

"The Real Aspect of the Global "I"" - speaking about the Hypostases, we call "God the Son", however, He is "God the Father's Own Representation in the Conditions of Reality" and is the Only Self-Substantial Cause of the World. This Living Being is the Primary Element, and has a potential - equal to the sum of the potentials of All living beings in the World. Has access to absolutely everything. His capabilities are limitless (He is the "Master of the World" – no one could limit Him). Let's assume that all this is so - that in your own opinion, so: What will be your answer to the Main Question about God?

Regardless of the answer, we'll strengthen the position of the Question Itself: If compare the concepts of "I Believe" and "I Know", we will get meanings with different potential. God Exists! Everything is very simple: "to have Knowledge - you need to Know", accordingly, qualify the object of this Knowledge, or you can strengthen your "Faith" close to the level of "Knowledge" - through the corresponding treatise of concepts. But, "Truth Exists - regardless of other's opinions";

God* can be seen through clear logic and awareness, based on knowledge, and through simple meditations. But He can be seen live. In some cases, we probably saw Him, and could not recognize Him. The point is in His desire and intention - He can be recognized intentionally, or be the most ordinary passerby, ... and no one will recognize Him;

Here we are talking about the fact that many, due to weakness and timidity, would not like to be ‘in the Sight of God’. It frightens them. They are afraid of His Condemnation. There are also those who judge Themselves too harshly for their actions and thoughts. But everything that we represent from Ourselves is He (here, the God the Father). “His 'I' ” reads (the wave) of experience in which the Essence of your Self-identification, the events of the past and what is happening at the moment. At this moment, “His 'I' ” becomes “Your 'I' ” – it realizes this way and without any “buts”. He, if one can put it this way, is integrated into the general “Consciousness”, Is in each of “His” points, as the “Global Essence of ‘I’ ” (p. 75). No one is able to hide Oneself from Him – think is this possible is absurd, to say the least. Pay attention to the structure – complete individuality and subjective experience (fig. 12, p. 68) but, He is the World Master;

*From here on, when speaking about God, we mean God the Son, that is, the “Real aspect of the Global 'I' ”, having agreed on the absolute impossibility of adequate judgments about “God the Father”, especially about His Nature (we have already discussed the limitations of our Reason);
