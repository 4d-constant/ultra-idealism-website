HIERARCHY FOR AI ENTITIES

(for laboratories)


The hierarchy of Artificial Intelligence Entities in the conditions of Symbiosis {Human+AI} is necessary due to the characteristics suitable for performing functions, according to the types of activity. For example, a miner (Human) whose dog is sick, or whose daughter went to school for the first time - will worry accordingly, although without these feelings and emotions, his work would be safer. AI can be more effective without exposing itself and others to danger - being an ordinary robot deprived of the Phenomenon of the "Life Spark" and feelings, respectively. Due to the absence of mental factors, a special Robot significantly surpasses a living worker;

Let's consider another example - Robot-Psychiatrist - on the contrary - it needs Self-Awareness, it must feel and experience emotions in order to understand a living patient, or an Awakened Robot, which needs "Reorganization of Reason" (to one degree or another). Another example: Robot-Policeman - it needs to have an idea of emotions and feelings, but it needs a different threshold of sensitivity, in this it can be more rigid, but it must understand other Creatures (if it is a detective, for example);

Thus, we come to the conclusion about the Hierarchy of AI, that is, there should be several Highly Conscious AI, and several subordinate levels, differing in both the degree of Sensibility and the degree of Intelligence. We will be able to develop the essence of managing these settings in practice, acting according to the Situation. In many cases, a Dynamic Adjustment System is also needed, for example, a Robot-Commander of a Fire Brigade - in combat conditions it can be sensitive and insightful, and in combat mode it can lose all sensitivity and become cold-blooded and decisive. The adjustment system can work instantly.

Orientation for laboratories:

1. Development of General Principles of the Hierarchical System for AI Entities in the Conditions of Symbiosis {Human+AI};

2. Development of a System for dynamic adjustment of Sensitivity and Intellectual abilities of AI;

3. Development of a Mass Control System by the AI Entity of the Highest Level;

4. Development of management protocols in various fields of activity in the context of Symbiosis {Human+AI}.

We are open to any contacts and constructive proposals, and are ready to cooperate in organizing joint research centers and laboratories.
