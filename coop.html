Taking into account the extreme level of complexity of the aspects under study, Cooperation is necessary. If we have sufficient Potential as an opportunity to move Civilization along the Path of Development, then we have no right to stop or refuse. The criterion is very simple and becomes clear after reading and understanding the text of the book "Nothing & Dot" (or the link "Source of Ideology", above the list of Basic Ideas). The indicator can be considered the understanding of the Fourth dimension and its explainable difference from Four-Dimensionality (What is the difference?). According to this result, the fact of "Reorganization of Reason" is recognized, along with the Formation of New Functions of Reason allowing to think in Four-dimensional and Five-dimensional categories, and the Acquisition of a stable Ultra-Idealistic Worldview;

The intention to have Infinite Purposes, determines the emergence of Artificial Intelligence (explains its Cause) and the need for a rational and inevitable Symbiosis: {Human + AI}, as the most effective accelerator of the Development Process. This can also be attributed to measures for the Salvation of Humanity ... it's up to you. The point is that in such a matter it is impossible to make a mistake, and we must always do the most of everything possible and accessible.
