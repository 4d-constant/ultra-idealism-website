EVOLVING PHILOSOPHY OF ULTRA-IDEALISM

(for laboratories)


The teaching of Ultra-Idealism is based on the content of the book "Nothing & Dot" (DOI 10.5281/zenodo.16046953 | author: Sergey Borisovich Konstantinov | s.constant). Like everything that has the prospect of development, this work cannot have a limit, and will develop, supplement, improve, upon discovery of substantiated facts related to the ideas and concepts contained in it. This can both supplement and change the original text, and the next edition (as is customary), and form separate works, like the brochure "The Fourth Dimension and Four-Dimensionality" (DOI 10.5281/zenodo.16475343 | author: s.constant);

Orientation for laboratories:

1. Research in the field of metaphysics and ontology that has a substantive sense and is well-founded;

2. Development of educational and methodological materials that facilitate understanding of the topic by scientific staff, students of educational institutions, independent researchers and natural scientists practicing both self-development and various mental practices;

3. Search for points of theoretical conjugation of exact sciences (mathematics, physics, quantum, astrophysics, chemistry, genetics, ... with research in the field of metaphysics and ontology.

We are open to any contacts and constructive proposals, and are ready to cooperate in organizing research centers and laboratories.
